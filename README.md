Reducing Stiffness and Pain
Muscle tightness and joint stiffness are common in paralysis. Stretching, range-of-motion exercises, and manual therapy help reduce discomfort and maintain flexibility.

Preventing Secondary Health Issues

Regular physiotherapy sessions improve blood circulation, reduce the risk of bedsores, prevent joint deformities, and support overall physical health.

Boosting Mental Well-being

Physical progress—no matter how small—plays a powerful role in mental recovery. Seeing improvements through physiotherapy for paralysis helps patients stay motivated and emotionally strong.
